Chef's Table

Being a chef is not easy.

If you forget to add an ingredient, you migth end up with a completely different dish and some angry customers.

The goal of the game is to help Chef Cocô get all the ingredients he needs to cook a dish in less than 1 minute.

Will you make it in time?

Instructions

Use the arrow keys to move Chef Cocô around the kitchen table.

Take care of the knives... If you step into one our Chef will die!

Last but not least, remember you only have 1 minute to pick up all the ingredients.


Click the button to start the game!
